Prayer of Salvation

Accept God’s free gift of Salvation

Do you want to have a personal relationship with God? We invite you to accept His free gift of salvation through His Son Jesus Christ.

For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life. (John 3:16)

You can pray the following prayer right now to ask God to come into your heart so that you can receive the miracle of new birth:

‘Almighty God, I come to you today. I confess that I am a sinner, and I cannot save myself. I believe that Jesus Christ came to this world, he died on the cross for my sins, and rose again triumphant on the third day. Right now I repent of my sins, and ask Jesus Christ to come into my life to be my Lord and personal Saviour. I believe according to your Word that I am now saved. Thank you Jesus for saving me from my sins. In Jesus Name, amen.’

If you have prayed the prayer above – congratulations! There is much rejoicing in heaven! Please let us know by filling and submitting the form.
